# Pricing & Free Tier Research

*Research conducted: January 2026*

## Competitor Pricing & Limits

| App | Free Tier Limit | Premium Price |
|-----|-----------------|---------------|
| **Greg** | 5 plants, no reminders | $29.99/year or $49.99 lifetime |
| **Blossom** | 2 reminders total | $4.99/mo or $29.99/year |
| **PlantIn** | 3 plants + limited IDs | ~$30/year |
| **Planta** | Unlimited plants, only watering reminders (no ID, care schedules, diagnosis) | $7.99/mo or $35.99/year |

### Two Main Strategies

1. **Limit by plant count** (Greg, PlantIn) — 3-5 plants free
2. **Limit by features** (Planta, Blossom) — unlimited plants, but core features paywalled

## Conversion Rate Benchmarks

- Typical freemium: **2-5%** convert to paid
- Consumer apps with good value gates: **5-10%**
- Free trials with payment upfront: **30-60%**
- The "80/20 rule": offer ~80% functionality free, reserve 20% high-value features for paid
- Optimal trial length: 7 days for monthly, 14 days for annual

## Competitor Complaints Analysis

### 1. Watering Schedules Are Wrong (Most Common)

> "If I watered on the recommended schedule all of my plants would be dead"

- Apps don't adapt to climate/humidity (desert users especially frustrated)
- Fixed schedules ignore pot size, soil type, light conditions
- No learning from user behavior over time

### 2. Plant ID Is Unreliable

> "Do not use the Plant ID. It will get it wrong 9 times out of 10"

- Misidentifications lead to wrong care advice
- Common plants sometimes not recognized
- Users forced to manually correct constantly

### 3. Aggressive Paywalls / Pricing Frustration

> "Every time we opened the app, it asked for payment and frustratingly hid the cancel button"

- Core features locked (even basic things like editing schedules)
- Sneaky free trial tactics
- Refund requests denied
- Free tier feels useless

### 4. Buggy & Poor UX

- Plants duplicating, disappearing, or resetting
- Can't undo accidental task completions
- Android experience worse than iOS
- Confusing UI (same colors for different actions)

### 5. No Real Personalization

> "The app doesn't seem to develop an algorithm over time"

- Doesn't learn from snoozes or user corrections
- One-size-fits-all advice
- Can't handle multiple plants in one pot

## Recommendations for Lily

### Free Tier Options

| Strategy | Free Limit | Rationale |
|----------|------------|-----------|
| Aggressive | 3 plants | Converts average users, matches PlantIn |
| Balanced | 5 plants | Matches Greg, covers average but not hobbyists |
| Growth-focused | 8-10 plants | Maximizes free adoption, only monetizes enthusiasts |

**Recommendation:** 5 plants feels like the sweet spot — generous enough for casual users, but hobbyists will outgrow it.

### Features to Paywall

- Unlimited plants (natural upgrade trigger)
- Disease/pest diagnosis
- Detailed care schedules beyond watering
- Export/history features
- Multiple rooms/locations

### Differentiation Opportunities

| Pain Point | Lily Opportunity |
|------------|------------------|
| Bad watering schedules | Smarter algorithms, climate-aware |
| Inaccurate plant ID | Confidence scores, easy corrections |
| Aggressive monetization | Fair, transparent free tier |
| Bugs/poor UX | Reliability + polish |
| No personalization | Learn from user behavior |
