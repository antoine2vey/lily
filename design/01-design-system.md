# Lily App - Part 1: Design System & Brand Identity

## Design Brief

Create a complete mobile app design system for **Lily**, a plant care management app. The design should feel **fresh, calming, and approachable** - like a digital greenhouse companion.

---

## Brand Identity

**App Name**: Lily
**Tagline**: "Your plants, thriving"

---

## Color Palette (Light & Greenish with Multiple Tones)

### Primary Colors
- **Sage Green**: #78A55A (primary actions, CTAs)
- **Mint**: #A8D5BA (secondary elements, backgrounds)
- **Forest**: #2D5A3D (text, icons, emphasis)

### Neutral Tones
- **Cream White**: #FAFDF7 (main background)
- **Soft Gray**: #F0F4EC (cards, containers)
- **Warm Gray**: #6B7B6E (secondary text)

### Accent Colors
- **Soft Yellow**: #F5E6A3 (sunlight indicators, achievements)
- **Coral**: #E8A598 (alerts, needs attention)
- **Sky Blue**: #A5C4D4 (water indicators)

### Health Status Colors
- **Thriving**: #4CAF50
- **Healthy**: #78A55A
- **Needs Attention**: #FFB74D
- **Sick**: #E57373
- **Recovering**: #64B5F6

---

## Typography

- **Headlines**: SF Pro Display (or similar) - Bold, rounded
- **Body**: SF Pro Text - Regular, clean
- **Accent**: A friendly rounded sans-serif for badges/labels

---

## Design Principles

1. Light, airy feel with plenty of white space
2. Soft shadows and rounded corners (16-24px radius)
3. Organic shapes and plant-inspired illustrations
4. Gentle gradients from mint to sage
5. Friendly empty states with plant illustrations
6. Micro-interactions for care actions (water droplet animations, leaf growth)

---

## Component Styles

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Sage green (#78A55A), white text, full rounded corners
- **Secondary**: White with sage border, sage text
- **Ghost**: Transparent with sage text
- **Disabled**: Soft gray background, muted text

### Input Fields
- **Default**: White background, soft gray border, rounded (12px)
- **Focus**: Sage green border, subtle shadow
- **Error**: Coral border, coral helper text
- **Success**: Green border with checkmark

### Cards
- White background
- Soft shadow (0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08))
- Rounded corners (16px)
- Optional mint gradient overlay for featured cards

### Badges/Pills
- Small rounded pills for status
- Health status uses corresponding colors
- Category badges use soft backgrounds with darker text

---

## Icon Style

- Outline style for inactive states
- Filled style for active states
- Rounded corners on all icons
- Consistent 24px base size
- Plant-themed custom icons where applicable

---

## Spacing System

- Base unit: 4px
- Common spacings: 8px, 12px, 16px, 24px, 32px, 48px
- Card padding: 16px
- Screen margins: 20px
- Section spacing: 24px

---

## Frame Specifications

- **Primary**: 390 x 844 (iPhone 14 Pro)
- **Large**: 428 x 926 (iPhone 14 Pro Max)
- **Android**: 360 x 800
- **Export**: 1x, 2x, 3x for assets
