## Visual Specs
- Card: white, 12px border-radius, shadow
- Image: aspect ratio 1:1, top portion, 8px border-radius top
- Name: 14px, medium, #1A1A1A, max 2 lines
- Status badge: top-right of image, absolute positioned
- Days indicator: "2 days" or "Today" or "Overdue"
- Favorite heart: top-left if favorited
