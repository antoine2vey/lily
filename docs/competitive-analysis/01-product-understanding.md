# Step 1 — Product Understanding

## What Lily Does

Lily is a **comprehensive plant care management app** that helps users track and maintain their plant collections with smart reminders, AI-powered diagnosis, care scheduling, and a social community. The tagline is **"Never lose a plant again."**

## Target Audience

- Indoor plant enthusiasts and hobbyists
- Beginner "plant parents" who struggle to keep plants alive
- Community-oriented plant lovers who want to share and learn
- Users seeking AI-powered expert plant care advice
- Bilingual users (English/French)

## Core Use Cases

1. **Plant inventory management** — catalog collections with photos, health status, room organization
2. **Smart care scheduling** — personalized watering, fertilizing, misting, repotting reminders
3. **Care logging** — record every action with photos and notes; build care history
4. **AI plant identification** — identify species instantly from photos
5. **AI chat & diagnosis** — ask plant-specific questions, get disease/pest treatment advice
6. **Gamification** — 15 achievement badges to build consistent care habits
7. **Social community** — follow users, showcase collections, send care nudges
8. **Plant delegation** — invite friends/family to co-care while traveling

## Summary (5-8 Bullet Points)

- **All-in-one plant care platform** combining inventory management, smart scheduling, AI diagnosis, and social community
- **AI-powered intelligence layer** with plant identification, conversational chat, disease diagnosis, and RAG-grounded knowledge base
- **Gamification engine** with 15 achievements, streaks, and engagement loops to build care habits
- **Social network for plant lovers** with follows, public profiles, nudges, and plant delegation
- **Subscription model** with generous trial (7 days) and competitive pricing ($4.99/mo, $29.99/yr)
- **Bilingual** (English/French) with full i18n support
- **Cross-platform** (iOS, Android) built on React Native/Expo with a Next.js marketing site
- **Mature backend** with Effect-based API, PostgreSQL, Redis, 11 background jobs, and admin dashboard

**AI-powered plant care management and social community app** — combining smart scheduling, AI identification/diagnosis, gamification, and social networking for indoor plant enthusiasts.
