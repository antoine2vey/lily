## Key Takeaways

1. **Lily's AI chat + RAG is the strongest differentiator** — no competitor has conversational AI for plant care
2. **Plant delegation is a category-creating feature** — zero competitors offer collaborative plant care
3. **Gamification is unmatched** — 15 achievements vs. zero from any competitor
4. **French-language support** opens a ~300M speaker market with no dominant player
5. **Biggest gap to close**: light meter (table stakes) and public plant encyclopedia (SEO moat)
6. **Pricing is competitive** — expand free tier from 5 to 15 plants to match Planta's generosity
7. **Fastest growth lever**: public plant encyclopedia + free web AI chat demo
