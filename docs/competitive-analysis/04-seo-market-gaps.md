## Content Gaps (Topics Not Well Covered)

### 1. AI-Powered Plant Care Content
- **Gap**: No competitor has content around "AI plant chat", "ask AI about plant problems", or "conversational plant care"
- **Opportunity**: Lily can own this entire content vertical as the first mover
- **Content ideas**: "How AI is changing plant care", "I asked AI about my dying monstera — here's what happened", "AI vs. Google for plant care advice"

### 2. Plant Delegation / Co-Care
- **Gap**: Zero content exists for "plant sitting apps", "delegate plant care", "plant care while traveling"
- **Opportunity**: Entirely uncontested keyword space
- **Content ideas**: "How to keep your plants alive while on vacation", "The plant sitter's guide", "Delegating plant care to friends"

### 3. Gamified Plant Care
- **Gap**: Very little content on gamification in plant care beyond academic papers
- **Opportunity**: Lily's 15-achievement system is a unique hook
- **Content ideas**: "Turn plant care into a game", "Plant care streaks that actually work", "Why gamification makes you a better plant parent"

### 4. Social Plant Communities
- **Gap**: Greg has some community content but no one owns "plant care social network"
- **Opportunity**: Lily's social features (follows, nudges, profiles) are differentiated
- **Content ideas**: "The plant parent community you didn't know you needed", "Share your plant collection online"

### 5. Species-Specific Care Guides (Bilingual)
- **Gap**: French-language plant care content is severely underserved
- **Opportunity**: Lily's i18n (en/fr) gives access to the entire Francophone market
- **Content ideas**: Bilingual care guides for top 100 houseplants, targeting "entretien [plante]", "comment arroser [plante]"
