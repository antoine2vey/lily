# Step 2 — Competitor Discovery

## Market Context

- **Plant Identification Apps Market**: ~$1.5B (2025), projected $2.9B by 2033 (8.8% CAGR)
- **Indoor Plants Market**: $13–23B (2025), growing 3.7–5.4% CAGR
- **Smart Gardening Apps Market**: rapidly expanding with AI, IoT, and AR integration
- **Key growth drivers**: urbanization, Gen Z engagement, sustainability trends, AI/ML advances
