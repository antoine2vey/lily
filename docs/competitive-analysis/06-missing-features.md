# Step 6 — Missing Core Features

## Table Stakes (Required to Compete)

These are features that market leaders have and users expect. Missing them creates friction for conversion.

| Feature | Who Has It | Impact | Effort | Priority |
|---------|-----------|--------|--------|----------|
| **Light meter** | Planta, Greg, Plantum, Blossom | High — users need to assess plant placement | Medium (camera + lux sensor API) | **P0 — Must have** |
| **Offline mode / local-first** | Partial across market (common complaint) | High — users in low-connectivity situations | High (local DB sync) | **P1 — Important** |
| **Snooze/reschedule reminders** | Expected by users, missing in many apps | Medium — UX quality of life | Low | **P0 — Quick win** |
| **Larger species database** | PictureThis (400K+), Planta (25K+) | Medium — affects ID accuracy and coverage | Medium (data acquisition) | **P1 — Ongoing** |
| **Onboarding plant quiz** | Blossom (skill level), several others | Medium — personalizes experience from day 1 | Low | **P0 — Quick win** |
| **Dark mode** | Standard expectation | Low-Medium — user comfort | Low (NativeWind theme) | **P0 — Quick win** |

## Critical Missing Features to Compete

| Feature | Why It Matters | Impact | Effort | Priority |

| **Seasonal care adjustments** | Planta and Greg auto-adjust for seasons; users expect this | High | Medium | **P1** |
| **Batch/bulk care actions** | Power users with 20+ plants need to mark multiple waterings at once | Medium | Low | **P0 — Quick win** |
| **Export/backup data** | Trust signal, reduces churn anxiety | Medium | Low | **P1** |
| **Widget (iOS/Android)** | Quick glance at today's care tasks without opening app | High (engagement) | Medium | **P1** |
